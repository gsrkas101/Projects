Being “blessed” with a strong economy and state of the art educational and social system, Singapore and Germany set benchmarks in today’s international educational market. By creating a healthy environment for a child to learn, SIE has developed a sophisticated pedagogical concept involving and encompassing the total development of each child: spiritually, morally, intellectually, socially, emotionally, and physically. The pedagogical concept is to be seen as the epitome of education and should be given priority ahead of the chosen curriculum. It is this pedagogical concept that helps a child realize his full potential and beauty in this world. This universal concept can be used with any curriculum existing in the educational market.
